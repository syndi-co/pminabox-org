The Problem

Your data has never been yours.

Vendor lock-in is the industry's oldest tax

Every property manager knows the drill. Your PMS holds your leases, your residents, your financials, your unit history — and the moment you want to move, migrate, back up, or build on top of it, you're quoted "extraction fees," stonewalled on exports, or handed a dump that's half the data you actually had. Decades of operator know-how, held hostage inside someone else's database.

This is the bane of everyone in the industry's existence. It has been for a long time. So we're doing something about it.

AI raised the stakes

AI only works when your data is accessible, structured, and yours to query. Property managers who can't freely move their PMS data can't put AI to work on it — they get whatever generic assistant their vendor decides to ship, on whatever timeline the vendor chooses, at whatever price the vendor lists.

If the industry doesn't build its own agentic harness, someone else will — and they'll lock us in again.

What we're building

An AI agentic harness for property management.
Open source. By the industry. For the industry.

PM in a Box is an open-source AI harness purpose-built for property management operations. "PM" stands for Property Management. The harness runs agents on your infrastructure, on your data, with skills and plugins contributed by operators and partners across the industry.

Enable AI with a one-click install to improve your property management business.

Skills & plugins from industry partners

Built once. Shared with everyone.

SEO / GEO (AI) optimized websites

Including hosting. Sites that rank in Google and get cited by AI answer engines.

Data backup & agentic retrieval

Continuous backup of your PMS data and an agent that can answer any question you ask of it.

Real-time bookkeeping

Daily profit & loss visibility for your portfolio — not a month behind, not a quarter behind.

AI-assisted frontline comms

Email, SMS, voice, and website chat — agents that triage, draft, and reply on your rules.
